DISA BANGLADESH

Development for Ideal Social Association in Bangladesh (DISA) is a non-profit, non-governmental, and non-political organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities by securing their basic rights and improving socio-economic conditions.

Our Background

Founded in 1997, DISA operates across 17 Upazilas in the Jamalpur region, focusing on marginalized groups â€” mostly poor, illiterate, and landless farmers and laborers. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to empower these communities through sustainable development projects, skill training, and social awareness.

Our Philosophy

  • Investing in human resources to enhance productivity
  • Promoting self-reliance through leadership and participation
  • Ensuring community involvement in planning and implementation
  • Fostering equitable access to opportunities regardless of background

Vision: A just and equitable society providing equal opportunities for all.

Mission: To build capacity and improve the quality of life for the poor in both urban and rural areas.
